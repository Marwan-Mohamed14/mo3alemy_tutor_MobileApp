Mo3alemy 📖

A tutor operating system for the Egyptian private tutoring market — not a marketplace, an infrastructure layer.

Mo3alemy gives private tutors a professional profile, booking calendar, payment logging, student roster, and parent communication tools — so tutors can run their own business without giving up their existing client relationships or paying commission on every session.


Think Shopify for tutors, not Amazon for tutors.




The Problem

Egypt has hundreds of thousands of private tutors who find students entirely through word of mouth, WhatsApp parent groups, and flyers. They have no professional profile, no scheduling system, no payment infrastructure, and no reviews. Parents searching for tutors face the same chaos from the other side.

Existing platforms (e.g. Superprof) solve this by taking high commission and commoditizing tutors into a marketplace listing. Mo3alemy solves it differently: give tutors the tools to run their own business professionally, let them keep their own students, and make the platform valuable enough that they never want to leave.


Who It's For


Tutors — Egyptian private tutors covering national curriculum, IG, AP, SAT, and university-level subjects
Students & Parents — K–12 and university students seeking verified, professional tutors
Admins — internal team managing verification, disputes, and platform health
